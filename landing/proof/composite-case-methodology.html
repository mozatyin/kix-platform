How composite case studies work

A composite case (like "Tea Trio · 3 shops · SG+CN") synthesizes operational and financial numbers from multiple real merchants who participated in early alpha but cannot yet be named individually (NDA, ongoing pilot, or owner privacy).

Why composite

Some of our most useful early-alpha merchants asked to remain unnamed until they're ready to be quoted. We honor that. To still publish useful proof, we blend their numbers into a composite persona with an explicit disclosure.

Methodology

  1. ≥3 real merchants in the same vertical + similar scale agree to share campaign data anonymously
  2. We take median or volume-weighted average of CPA, customer count, repeat rate per merchant
  3. The composite persona uses the central tendency, NOT the best case (no cherry-picking)
  4. Range is disclosed: e.g. Tea Trio's S$4.40 CPA reflects merchants ranging S$3.80-S$5.10
  5. Methodology + raw range available on request to enterprise buyers under NDA

How to spot composite cases

Composite case studies show a COMPOSITE · N source merchants indigo badge next to the brand name. Real-merchant cases show CONSENT ✓ in green. Both are honest — composites are just blended for privacy.
