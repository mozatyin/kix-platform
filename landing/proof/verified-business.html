What is a Verified Business?

Proof artifact for claim verified_business_definition

"Verified Business" is the KiX trust tier required for the S$499/mo Pro plan and pay-as-you-go CPA model. It is not a paywall — it's anti-fraud. ~24h end-to-end.

  1. Business registration: upload your ACRA (SG) / SSM (MY) / 营业执照 (CN) certificate
  2. Bank statement (last 90 days): any business bank account · we mask account numbers
  3. Owner ID: NRIC / IC / passport · we hash + delete after verification
  4. 1 transaction proof: a receipt / POS record showing the business is live
  5. Manual review: ~24h (faster on business days) · auto-approve for AU/SG/HK · WhatsApp founder for ambiguous cases

Why this exists: stops bad actors from using KiX to wash CPA budget into their own fake "customer" accounts. Without verification we'd be a money-laundering risk.
