Audit log architecture · regulator-grade

Proof artifact for claim audit_log_architecture. Designed for PIPL §51 + GDPR Art 30 + PDPA-SG §11 accountability obligations.

Storage

  • Primary: PostgreSQL · table audit_log (migration 0007_audit_log.py) · WAL-archived
  • Retention: 7 years (regulatory baseline) · auto-archive to S3 Glacier at year 2
  • Immutability: append-only · DELETE forbidden at DB-role level · time-series hash chain
  • Region pinning: SG tenant data in ap-southeast-1 only · HK in ap-east-1 · CN in cn-shanghai-1

Regulator access

PDPC / IMDA can request audit log extracts under Court Order or PDPA §26D. Response SLA: ≤ 48 hours for narrow queries (single kid, date range < 30d); ≤ 5 business days for broader queries. Format: CSV + signed hash + SOC2 chain-of-custody form.
