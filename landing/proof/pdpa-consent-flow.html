PDPA-SG consent flow · 15 scopes · granular

Proof artifact for claim pdpa_consent_flow. Implements PDPA-SG §13-15 (consent obligation) + §16 (withdraw obligation) + §17 (purpose limitation).

15 consent scopes (granular · independent grant/withdraw)

  1. profile · basic ID (name + phone) · required for redemption
  2. email · email contact · optional · only used for transactional + receipt
  3. marketing_push · push notifications · explicit opt-in · default OFF
  4. marketing_sms · SMS marketing · explicit opt-in · default OFF
  5. geolocation_active · location when actively playing game · session-scoped
  6. geolocation_passive · background location · explicit opt-in · default OFF
  7. cross_brand_share · KiX may show your activity to OTHER brands · default OFF
  8. analytics_personal · personalized analytics · opt-in default OFF
  9. analytics_aggregate · anonymized aggregate · opt-in default ON
  10. third_party_share · share with named partners · per-partner explicit opt-in
  11. cdp_export · merchant's own CDP (Salesforce / Segment / etc.) · per-merchant grant
  12. research · anonymized research (academic + Mozat internal) · default OFF
  13. regulatory_required · cannot be withdrawn (KYC/AML/audit) · always ON
  14. minor_data · only granted via parent/guardian flow · age-gated
  15. biometric · device fingerprint for fraud · always ON (anti-fraud carve-out)

Withdraw in 1 click

Settings → Privacy → Consent scopes → toggle off any scope. Effect within 5 minutes (system-wide). Withdraw is logged with timestamp + UI surface in audit_log (immutable record).
