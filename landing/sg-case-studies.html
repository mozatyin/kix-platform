What broke along the way (honest log)

  • Day 2: Uncle Ng's cousin tried to game the system 14× from one phone — caught + blocked (fraud heuristic worked).
  • Day 8: Mandarin character set broke in voucher print — fixed Day 9.
  • Day 23: Stall helper redeemed 6 vouchers as "test" — credited back, training fix shipped.
  • Day 41: CPA spiked +18% one week — root cause: cross-pool oversaturation. Adjusted bid floor.
  • Day 90: Uncle Ng signed scale-to-3-stalls extension. Numbers above are net of all 5 incidents.
