What a 90-day enterprise pilot looks like

No 12-month commitment. No prepaid floor. Just three milestones, three checkpoints, an honest exit at any of them.

  • Day 0: Sign 1-page MSA + DPA, choose 2-5 pilot outlets, $30/outlet founder credit auto-applied

  • Day 90: Full retention + LTV + ROAS comparison vs your pre-KiX baseline. Decision point: scale, pivot, or exit cleanly
  • Anytime: Pause campaigns, export all data, cancel — no clawback, no clause
