POS · INTEGRATION GUIDE

"Will POS integration be a 3-month IT nightmare?"

Short answer: no. Most merchants ship in 15 minutes using zero-IT manual redeem. If you want a deeper sync to your POS for tracked-transaction CPS, we've done it in 1–2 days with StoreHub / Square / Shopify / Stripe Terminal.

🚫 The myth (other loyalty SaaS)

"Sign a 12-month contract, schedule 6 calls with our integration team, custom dev to your POS, allocate a sprint from your IT, wait 3 months." We've heard this story from every alpha merchant who came from Eber / Klaviyo / Salesforce Loyalty.

✅ The reality (KiX)

KiX is designed to not need POS integration. Customers redeem via QR code at the counter — your staff reads a 4-digit code, types it in our portal (or scans). Wallet charges and CPA tracking work without ever touching your POS. POS deep-sync is opt-in only, for merchants who want auto-tracked CPS (revenue-share pricing).

Zero-IT path

Manual redeem

15 min · no POS needed

How 90% of our alpha-cohort merchants run today. Print a QR sticker for your counter. Staff types the redemption code into the portal app on their phone.

  • Works with ANY cash register / cash drawer
  • No POS access needed
  • Fraud prevention built-in (single-use codes, device-bound)
  • Charges your wallet on confirmed redeem only
  • Best for: F&B counters, hawker stalls, small retail
Deep-sync path

POS-tracked CPS

1–2 days · one of 5 providers

For chain operators who want revenue-share billing (5–15% CPS instead of fixed CPA), so KiX charges only on the actual basket size. POS sends purchase event back.

  • Native: StoreHub, Square, Shopify, Stripe Terminal, Toast
  • Generic: any POS that supports webhooks (n8n, Zapier as bridge)
  • Setup wizard pre-builds the webhook URL + signing secret
  • Tested with 5 providers across 8 SG / 3 MY archetypes
  • Best for: chain operators wanting accurate ROAS reporting

FAQ — what chain operators actually ask

Can I start with manual redeem and add POS sync later?

Yes — that's the recommended path. Most merchants pilot 30 days on manual + CPA to validate the customer-acquisition story, then opt into POS deep-sync only if they want CPS revenue-share pricing. No code change, just toggle in portal.

Will my IT team need to be involved?

Manual path: zero IT involvement. Just a printed QR + staff training on the redeem button. Deep-sync path: 1 IT person × half-day for the supported providers (StoreHub/Square/Shopify/Stripe). The setup wizard pre-generates the webhook URL + signing secret. We provide the JSON schema for the inbound order event.

What happens if my POS is offline / network down?

Manual redeem keeps working — code lookup happens server-side on KiX, not your POS. For deep-sync providers, we buffer events server-side and retry; if your POS is down >24h, we fall back to manual redeem mode automatically and alert ops.

How is "tracked transaction" defined for CPS pricing?

A unique consumer who (a) played a KiX game, (b) registered with phone/email, AND (c) the POS reported a Purchase event tied to that consumer's redemption code within 30 days. All three required. POS event source = StoreHub / Square / Shopify / Stripe Terminal webhook OR manual confirm. Same definition across pricing page.

What about my legacy POS (e.g. old Excel + cash register)?

Manual path works perfectly — no POS, no problem. If you want to migrate to a webhook-supporting POS later, we'll help you scope it. We won't sell you our own POS; we partner with the existing players.

I run 100 outlets. Can I onboard them all at once?

For manual redeem: yes — bulk QR generation in portal, ship printed materials to all outlets, train staff via 5-min video. Live across 100 outlets in <1 week. For deep-sync: roll out one POS provider at a time; we've done 40-outlet StoreHub bulk-setup in 3 days during alpha.

Halal / PDPA / data residency for MY operators?

POS data flows are transit-only (we don't store full basket details unless you opt in). Hashed customer phone/email for attribution match. SG-residency option available; MY-residency Q1 2027.
