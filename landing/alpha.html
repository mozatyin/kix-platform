FAQ

Why an alpha programme?

We're building KiX with merchants, not for them. The alpha cohort gets direct line to the team — every feature on the roadmap traces back to a real conversation with an alpha member.

What happens after 90 days?

Your trial converts to a regular STARTER plan unless you cancel. Alpha members get a discount that carries forward — we'll confirm it in writing before the trial ends.

How much time will this take?

One 15-minute weekly check-in for the first month, then monthly. Plus a 30-second NPS survey each month. We respect your time.

What if KiX isn't a fit?

No charge, no obligation. You walk away with whatever data you exported and our thanks for the feedback.
