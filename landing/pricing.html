Questions you're probably asking

What if I want to leave?

No contract. Cancel anytime. Your campaigns stay live until the budget runs out, then auto-pause. Your data is exportable via CSV or API at any moment.

How is "delivered customer" defined?

A delivered customer is a unique consumer who (a) completed a KiX-generated game, (b) registered as a customer (phone or email), and (c) made a tracked first transaction with your brand within 30 days. You only pay when all three happen.

What does CPS actually cost?

You choose. The 5%-15% range is auction-set — higher bids win more auctions but cost more per customer. Most F&B merchants set 5-8%. Most beauty/retail set 10-15%. You can change anytime.

Is "First 100 free" really permanent?

Yes. Founding-merchant slots have 0% take rate forever — not a 90-day trial, not a discount. We use this to seed each market's network effects. Once a country's 100 slots fill, new merchants pay normal CPA/CPS.

What payment methods do you accept?

Singapore: PayNow + credit card. Asia: GrabPay, Alipay, WeChat Pay, OVO. Global: Stripe (cards). You only get charged after each campaign delivers customers — no monthly bills.

Can I see real merchant numbers?

Yes — 5 Singapore F&B businesses are currently in the alpha cohort with real campaigns running. See SG case studies → (Heng Heng Kopi, Brew Lab, Aminah's Halal Hut, Common Man Coffee, Old Chang Kee). What about enterprise contracts?

Large brands (50+ stores) can request custom SLAs, API/SFTP integration, and 12-month commitment terms. Same per-result pricing, just with predictable invoicing.
