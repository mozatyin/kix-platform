1. Who we are and our role

The data controller for personal data we collect through the KiX consumer app, the merchant portal and our public websites is KiX (Singapore) Pte. Ltd. When we process personal data on behalf of an enterprise merchant under a written data-processing arrangement, we act as a data processor for that merchant and the merchant is the controller. The terms of that processing are set out in our Data Processing Addendum.

2. Data we collect

CategoryExamples
Account dataName, email, phone, password hash, business name and registration (merchants), preferred language, country.
Behavioural dataGames played, offers viewed, taps, dwell time, swipe direction, retention signals, in-app feed interactions.
Financial dataWallet balance, top-up history, redemption history, payout details (merchants). Card details are handled by Stripe and never stored by KiX.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive categories of data (health, biometrics, political opinion, etc.) and do not require them to use the service.

3. Why we collect it (purposes)

  • Provide the service — create accounts, render games, redeem offers, fulfil merchant campaigns.
  • Billing — process top-ups, payouts and invoicing.
  • Fraud, abuse and safety — detect bot activity, payment fraud, account takeover, and enforce the Acceptable Use Policy.
  • Personalisation — rank feed items, match users to nearby merchants and tune recommendations.
  • Analytics and product — measure engagement, debug, improve game templates and platform reliability.
  • Compliance — meet legal obligations including tax, accounting and lawful requests.

4. Lawful basis

Where required (most clearly under EU/UK GDPR, but also reflected in PDPA, PIPL and CCPA), we rely on the following bases:

BasisWhen it applies
Performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))Operating your account, processing offers, fulfilling top-ups.
Legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))Fraud detection, network and information security, basic analytics, product improvement — balanced against your rights and freedoms.
Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a); PIPL Art. 13(1); CCPA opt-in for minors)Precise location, marketing emails, non-essential cookies, processing of children’s data above local thresholds.
Legal obligation (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c))Tax records, anti-money-laundering checks where applicable, response to lawful requests.
Vital interests / public taskRare — emergency safety reports.

Under China PIPL, separate consent is collected where required (sensitive data, cross-border transfer, automated decisions with significant effect). Under California CCPA/CPRA, we honour “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” signals; KiX does not sell personal information for monetary consideration.

5. Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as we have a lawful purpose. Retention reflects the regional compliance rules baked into our platform:

RegionDefault retention for account & transaction records
Singapore (PDPA)Up to 7 years from last account activity (tax / accounting backstop).
European Union (GDPR)Variable; tied to purpose. Account: life of account + up to 2 years. Billing: 6–10 years per Member-State rules.
China (PIPL)Default 3 years for personal data; sectoral law may extend specific categories.
United StatesPer state law; CCPA disclosures explain category-level retention.
Indonesia / Thailand / Vietnam / Philippines / India / BrazilPer local PDP/LGPD/PDPA equivalents; minimum 12 months for transaction logs, longer where local tax law requires.

Behavioural events used solely for ranking and analytics are aggregated or deleted within 24 months unless tied to an active account.

6. Third-party sharing

We share personal data only with the categories of recipient listed below and only as needed for the purpose stated:

  • Payment processing — Stripe (card processing, payouts; PCI DSS Level 1).
  • Cloud infrastructure — Amazon Web Services and regional providers (hosting, storage, CDN).
  • Maps and geocoding — Google Maps Platform.
  • AI model provider — Anthropic, for limited model inference where features require it; inputs are minimised, do not include card data, and (per our enterprise agreement) are not used to train third-party models.
  • Analytics and observability — error and performance monitoring providers under contract.
  • Merchants — when you redeem an offer, the relevant merchant receives the minimum data needed to fulfil it.
  • Law-enforcement and regulators — when required by valid legal process.
  • Corporate transactions — in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, subject to equivalent privacy commitments.

We do not sell personal information.

7. International transfers and data residency

KiX uses regional infrastructure to keep personal data close to where users are. Personal data from users in China stays in China consistent with PIPL data-residency expectations and is not transferred out of China except where a recognised mechanism (separate consent, security assessment, standard contract) applies.

For transfers from the EU/EEA, the United Kingdom or Switzerland to third countries that do not have an adequacy decision, we rely on the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses (2021), supplemented by appropriate technical and organisational measures and a transfer-impact assessment.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access — receive a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that inaccurate or incomplete data be updated.
  • Deletion / erasure — request deletion (subject to retention obligations).
  • Portability — receive a copy in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection / restriction — object to certain processing or ask us to restrict it.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, at any time and without affecting prior lawful processing.
  • Complain — to your local data-protection authority (PDPC in Singapore; your DPA in the EU; CAC in China; CA AG / state AG in the US).

To exercise a right, write to dpo@letskix.com. We respond within statutory timelines (30 days under GDPR / PDPA; longer where complexity requires extension and you are notified).

9. Cookies and similar technologies

Refer to our Cookie Policy for the full list. We use essential cookies for session and CSRF, functional cookies for locale and preferences, analytics cookies (with consent in the EU) and advertising cookies (with consent where required).

10. Children’s privacy

KiX is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. Where local law sets a higher digital-consent age, we apply that age (GDPR default of 16 in the EU). If we learn we have collected personal data from a child below the applicable age without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly. Parents and guardians can contact dpo@letskix.com.

11. Security

We apply encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest, principle-of-least-privilege access controls, audit logging, periodic access reviews, incident-response runbooks and vendor risk reviews. No system is 100% secure; we will notify regulators and affected users of a notifiable breach within statutory timelines (72 hours under GDPR; PDPA-equivalent in Singapore; and per local law elsewhere).

12. Region-specific notices

Singapore (PDPA)

KiX has appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) reachable at dpo@letskix.com. We collect personal data for the purposes listed in Section 3 and may rely on deemed consent for processing that is reasonably necessary to provide a service you requested. Cross-border transfers comply with §26 of the PDPA. You may complain to the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC).

European Union / EEA / UK (GDPR)

Controller: KiX (Singapore) Pte. Ltd. EU representative (when designated): published on this page once appointed. Lawful bases are as listed in Section 4. You may complain to your local supervisory authority. We do not engage in solely automated decisions producing legal or similarly significant effects without meaningful human oversight.

China (PIPL)

Personal information of users located in China is stored in China. Separate consent is requested where required (sensitive data, automated decisions, transfers). You can exercise PIPL rights by writing to dpo@letskix.com. Complaints may be filed with the Cyberspace Administration of China (CAC).

United States (CCPA / CPRA and state laws)

California residents may submit requests to know, delete, correct, limit the use of sensitive personal information, and opt out of sharing for cross-context behavioural advertising. KiX does not sell personal information for monetary consideration. We honour Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals as opt-out requests. Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas and other states with comprehensive privacy laws have analogous rights.
