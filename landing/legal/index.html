How these documents fit together

The Terms of Service are the master agreement. The Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy are notices made under those Terms. The Refund Policy and Acceptable Use Policy are operational policies referenced from the Terms. The DPA attaches to the Terms when you sign up as an enterprise customer that needs a written data-processing arrangement (mandatory under GDPR Article 28 and equivalents).

Where these documents conflict with a signed master service agreement between KiX and an enterprise customer, that signed agreement prevails.

Locale notes
